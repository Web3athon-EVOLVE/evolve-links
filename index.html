EVOLVE: A Education and Finance DApp
×
A Learn-To-Earn & community pooling platform to strengthen financial literacy and mental health; supporting BIPOC women globally to build generational wealth.

It is designed to teach BIPOC women budgeting, net worth, financial wellness, and web3 concepts through cliturally competent financial learning tools and mindset strengthening for financial well- being.

The user is rewarded the SEVOLVE token for completing education modlies, quizzes, and wellness activities. They can deposit a percentage of earned rewards into a decentralized community pool "Evolution Pool" to practice building individual and community wealth through collective investing and APY.

EVOLVE is for Black, Indigenous, and People of Color (BIPOC) Women. We focus on:
  • Women wanting to grow their net worth and are curious to learn how
  • Women wanting to shift their mindset so they can 'get out of their own way' to build wealth;
  • Women willing to take the chance to be proactive in increasing their financial literacy as they 'learn and earn' in a safe web3 environment.
